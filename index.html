About

Rod Barrett is an independent music artist in Lancaster, PA
where he became a self taught producer with 3 years of experience
producing in his own home for himself and his friends. He also
currently has several projects he is a part of within Lancaster
City as well as a classic rock cover band called KIXTART based
out of Ephrata, PA where he enjoys performing some of his favorite
songs growing up.
