About Me

Rod Barrett is an independent music artist who grew up in
Lancaster, PA where he learned to play the guitar at a
young age. As time went on, Rod continued through school
to get into Penn State University to get his Computer
Engineering degree and pursue Software Engineering. After
some time (and a few guitars later) he realized his passion
in music was too much to keep to himself and started producing
his own music. Some of Rod Barrett's biggest influences are
Eddie Van Halen, Jimi Hendrix, Randy Rhoads, Andy Timmons,
and David Gilmour.
